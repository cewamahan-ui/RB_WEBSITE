GUMBO BONEZ N' ALL (2023)

The debut project from Kenyan collective 'The Rugbones'. The EP's title 'GUMBO' serves as a metaphor for a melting pot of diverse talents, blending musical styles and experiences that will captivate listeners. With this ambitious project, The Rugbones intend to redefine boundaries, push the envelope of creativity, and establish themselves as true innovators in the Kenyan rap scene.
